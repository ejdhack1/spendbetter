# Ethical Spending Guide  
## Avoiding Corporations That Backed Trump-Era or Anti-Democratic Policies

**Purpose:**  
Help consumers make everyday purchasing decisions that avoid financially supporting corporations linked to:
- Trump-aligned political funding
- Election denial
- ICE enforcement and surveillance
- DEI rollbacks
- Anti-labor or anti‚Äìcivil rights behavior

This guide emphasizes **practical substitutes**, not just boycotts.

---

## How Companies Are Evaluated

Corporations are flagged using publicly verifiable signals:

### Primary Signal Types

- Political donations to Trump or election deniers
- Corporate PAC funding to anti-democratic lawmakers
- Federal contracts with ICE, DHS, or surveillance agencies
- Public rollback or elimination of DEI programs
- Anti-union actions or labor violations
- Public support for culture-war or anti‚Äìcivil rights policies

### Source Types

- Federal Election Commission (FEC) data  
- OpenSecrets.org political finance database  
- USAspending.gov federal contract database  
- AP News, Reuters, ProPublica reporting  
- CREW (Citizens for Responsibility and Ethics in Washington)  
- Grab Your Wallet Alliance  
- ICE Out of My Wallet campaign

## Design Philosophy

This guide follows three principles:

1. **Evidence-based** ‚Äî all claims trace to public sources  
2. **Practical** ‚Äî always offer realistic substitutes  
3. **Transparent** ‚Äî scoring logic and sources are visible

This is not a boycott list.  
It is a **values-aligned decision support tool** for consumers.
